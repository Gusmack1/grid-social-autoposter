Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

Grid Social ("we", "us", "our") operates a social media management platform at gridsocial.co.uk. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account or are invited to our platform, we collect your name and email address.

Social Media Account Data

When you connect social media accounts through our platform, we collect:

  • Facebook: Page ID, page name, page access token, fan count
  • Instagram: Business Account ID, username, follower count
  • LinkedIn: Organisation ID, organisation name, access token
  • Twitter/X: Account credentials for posting
  • TikTok: Account credentials for posting
  • Google Business Profile: Location ID and access credentials

We collect only the minimum permissions needed to schedule and publish posts on your behalf.

Content Data

Post captions, images, videos, and scheduling information you provide through our platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Publishing scheduled social media posts to your connected accounts
  • Displaying your account connection status on our dashboard
  • Monitoring token health to ensure uninterrupted service
  • Sending you notifications about post approvals and account status

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

All social media access tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption. Tokens are decrypted only at the moment of publishing, inside secure server-side functions.

Our platform is hosted on Netlify with encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 with random salts.

We do not store access tokens in environment variables, source code, or client-side storage.

4. Data Retention

  • Access tokens: Stored encrypted until you disconnect the account or we remove the client
  • Post history: Retained for reporting purposes until the client is deleted
  • Account data: Retained while you have an active account

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. When a client is removed from our platform:

  • All encrypted access tokens are permanently deleted
  • Post history and scheduling data are permanently deleted
  • Account connection records are permanently deleted

6. Facebook and Instagram Data

We access Facebook and Instagram data through Meta's Graph API. Specifically:

  • We request pages_manage_posts to publish content to your Facebook Page
  • We request pages_read_engagement to read post performance
  • We request pages_show_list to show your available pages during connection
  • We request instagram_basic to read your Instagram account information
  • We request instagram_content_publish to publish content to your Instagram account

We do not access your personal Facebook profile, friends list, or private messages. We only access data related to Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts you explicitly authorise.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Netlify: Hosting and serverless functions (USA)
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): Social media APIs
  • LinkedIn: Social media API
  • GitHub: Source code hosting and media storage

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Data portability
